Reading and Responses

Professors will provide prompts in each section’s Slack channel based on the readings. To gain credit, please respond in the channel for that prompt by class time the following week, and add something else to someone else’s response in a thread.

Our goal is to inspire debate and discussion around the role of code in society. We may shift the requirements as the semester progresses if new forms of debate prove to be more enlightening. Our Reading Responses would usually be a discussion in class, so we’re attempting to move that online.
